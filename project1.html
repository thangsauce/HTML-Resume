Symphony Flow

Project Description

  • The project is a digital scheduling, registration, and billing system that replaces LBMA’s manual paper-based process, reducing scheduling conflicts, administrative workload, and response delays for students, parents, and staff
  • Aims to achieve 95% user satisfaction, a 25% reduction in administrative tasks, and a 15% increase in consistent revenue by improving efficiency, accuracy, and overall user experience

Skills Learned

  • Define project scope, manage changes, and ensure alignment with goals, timeline, and budget
  • Testing, verification, and implementing quality metrics to ensure a reliable, user-friendly digital system
  • Gather requirements, engage users, and communicate effectively with team members and clients to ensure project success
